✨ The Excitement of the Tour

Our educational tour to Baguio filled me with excitement, especially because it was my first time visiting the city. As soon as we arrived, the cool breeze and beautiful mountain views made the trip feel unforgettable. I was amazed by every place we visited, from the historical spots to the colorful markets that showed the rich culture of Baguio. Exploring the city with my classmates made the experience even more meaningful and fun. Each moment felt new and special, and I found myself learning not only from the places we toured but also from the people around me.
